Camp Details

Camp Details

  • Tuition: $350.00/week. ($280.00 the week of July 4th due to no camp on July 4th.) Tuition is non-refundable after June 1st.
  • Early Bird Discount: Use coupon code CAMPBUNDLE20 when enrolling in two camps and CAMPBUNDLE50 when enrolling in three camps.
  • Location: Located at our Redmond studio - 13200 Old Redmond Rd #105, Redmond, WA 98052.
  • What to Wear: Dancers should wear comfortable or athletic clothes. Dance shoes (ballet, jazz) are welcome, or they can dance barefoot.
  • What to Pack: Send a large snack/lunch and a water bottle. Our studio is nut-free.
  • Pick Up/Drop Off: Morning drop-off: 9:00 AM, pick-up: 12:00 PM. Afternoon drop-off: 1:00 PM, pick-up: 4:00 PM.
  • Ages & Levels: Campers will be divided into age or skill-based groups.
  • Potty Training: All campers must be potty trained.

CAMP SCHEDULE:

  • 9:00 - 9:15 AM - Camper Drop-Off
  • 9:15 - 9:30 AM - Camp Assembly
  • 9:30 - 10:10 AM - Camp Craft
  • 10:10 - 10:30 AM - Snack Time + Story Time
  • 10:30 - 11:30 AM - Dance Class!
  • 11:30 - 11:50 AM - Camp Share
  • 11:50 AM - 12:00 PM - Clean-Up Time
  • 12:00 PM - Camper Pick-Up
  • Fridays Only: 11:30 AM - 12:00 PM - End of Camp Performance
