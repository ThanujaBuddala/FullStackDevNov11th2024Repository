Welcome to Sai Dance Academy!

Sai Dance Academy is a welcoming and inclusive studio where anyone can learn to dance. We provide high-quality dance training that builds technical excellence, creativity, healthy minds, and healthy bodies.

The Sai Dance Difference:

Welcome All Dancers

  • All ages (2yrs - senior), levels, identities, and aspirations (recreational to professional) are welcome.
  • We offer many styles of dance so dancers can select the movement styles they love.
  • We are non-competitive; all abilities are welcome!
  • We offer Adaptive Dance Classes (ADC) for students with disabilities or neurodivergence.

Evolve Physical Training & Mental Health Support

  • To provide safe and effective training, our staff has certifications in Progressing Ballet Technique, Pointe Safe, Youth Protection Advocates in Dance, Youth Mental Health First Aid, Integrated Movement Therapy, Brain-Compatible Dance Education, and more.
  • These programs are built into our curriculum.

Educate on Diversity in Dance & Increase Representation

  • We celebrate heritage/identity months and Body Pride Week through in-class and online education.
  • Our Lobby Library features dance books crossing different cultures and identities.
  • Our staff has diverse identities, allowing students to see people like themselves in dance.

Create Accessibility Through Financial Help & Outreach

  • We are official partners with the City of Redmond, providing outreach dance classes.
  • We offer tuition assistance for families in need
  • We offer a borrow bin where students may ballet & tap shoes

Build Inclusivity Into Our Practices

  • Dress codes and costuming are gender-agnostic
  • Pronouns are used
  • Instructors teach to all learning styles (Aural, Visual, Linguistic, Physical, Logical, Emotional, Social, Solitary)

Have Fun & Build Community!

  • We hire instructors who have a deep knowledge of dance but also LOVE teaching and bringing joy to the classroom
  • We host community events (COVID permitting) to build our community, shared experience, and a sense of belonging
