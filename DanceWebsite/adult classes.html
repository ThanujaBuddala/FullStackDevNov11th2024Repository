Class Descriptions

Jazz

Bring energy and creativity to the floor in our Adult Jazz class! This fun and high-energy class combines classic jazz techniques with contemporary styles. You’ll work on strength, flexibility, and coordination while learning sharp, expressive movements and dynamic choreography. Perfect for all levels—whether you're new to dance or coming back for more!

Studio Hip-Hop

Learn the fundamentals of street hip-hop in a studio setting. This class covers bounces, rolls, pops, locks, and breaking, all set to the latest hip-hop music. Whether you join our Beginning or Intermediate class, you’ll learn choreography and dive into the culture of hip-hop dance.

Contemporary

Explore the artistry of movement in our Adult Contemporary class. This style blends modern dance, jazz, and ballet, focusing on fluidity, creativity, and emotional expression. Suitable for all levels, you'll connect with the music and express yourself through dynamic choreography and improvisation.

Ballet

Build strength and flexibility while mastering classical ballet techniques. Ballet classes include barre and center work to improve your form and technique. Ballet shoes (or socks for intermediate/advanced dancers) are required.

Pointe - Beginning

Take your ballet to the next level with our Beginning Pointe class. For adults with a solid ballet foundation, this class focuses on developing the strength, technique, and alignment needed to dance en pointe. Instruction is tailored to help you progress safely and confidently. New Students: Instructor permission is required to enroll. Please email

Tap Dancing

Learn the basics of tap dancing while building rhythm and timing. Let go and immerse yourself to the variety of music our Tap classes offer, including Broadway, swing jazz, classical tunes, and popular hits. Tap shoes are required.
