Tuition & Studio Policies

  • 45-minute class: $24.00
  • 1 hour class: $26.00
  • 1.25 hour & Adult Classes: $26.00
  • 1.5 hour & Adult Classes: $26.00
  • 2 hours per week: $48.00
  • 2.5 hours per week: $60.00
  • 3 hours per week: $69.00
  • Adult Drop-In Class: $30.00

How is Tuition billed?


  • Tuition is auto-charged upon registration and on the 1st of each month following.
    Monthly tuition amounts reflect how many classes occur during that month. Please note: December tuition is charged on Nov. 1st and June tuition is charged on May 1st due to December and June being partial months. Students may not attend class if an account is past due.
  • Enrollment in Auto-Pay in the Class Portal is required for registration.

What if I enroll part-way through the month?


  • Pro-Rated Tuition:Tuition will be prorated if you join part-way through a month for the remaining classes in that month.

What is your refund policy?


  • Tuition is non-refundable: Once tuition is charged, it is non-refundable. If a student misses a class, you may be able to schedule a make-up class, but it is not guaranteed. Please see our make-up class policy below for details.
  • We have case-by-case exceptions, which need to be approved by the Office Manager, please inquire with our office if you have an extenuating circumstance.

What other fees should I expect?


  • A $35 Annual Registration Fee(per family) is due at registration and is non-refundable. Registration fees cover our music licensing fees, insurance, and studio equipment maintenance, which increase costs for each student. Registration fees are good for the school year (September 1st - August 31st) and renew when you register for classes occurring in the next school year.
