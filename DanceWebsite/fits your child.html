Preschool Dance Classes (Ages 1.5-5)
REGISTER OR BOOK A FREE TRIAL

Introduce your child to the world of dance!

Our preschool classes are filled with movement, dancing, music, and joy! Our littlest movers will learn foundational dance skills, explore weekly dance concepts like size, speed, and rhythm through multi-sensory activities, and develop their creativity and independence. Our expert instructors love working with preschool-age dancers and are trained in our early childhood development-based preschool curriculum.

Class Levels & Descriptions

Dancin' Tots (1.5-4yrs w/ Caregiver)

Exploring Movement with a Caregiver

Join us for a fun and expressive class with your dancer. Our program is designed to challenge your little ones' coordination while building their confidence through creative movement, obstacle courses, multi-sensory dance explorations, and free dance.


Dancers must be 1.5 years old by their first class and be accompanied by a caregiver.

Creative Ballet & Tap (3-4yrs)

Joyfully Dancing Independently

Experience the excitement of dancing freely in a caring and supportive environment. Dancers learn beginning dance and tap skills using props, improvisation, movement activities, obstacle courses, and free dance.



Dancers must be 3 years old by their first class and be able to dance and follow instructions independently of their caregiver.

Creative Hip-hop & Tap (3.5-5yrs)

Move, Groove, Wiggle and Tap

Get ready to get all the wiggles out in this hip-hop and tap-based dance party. Dancers learn foundational hip-hop and tap skills to build their creativity and musicality through weekly dance concept explorations, movement activities, and obstacle courses.


Dancers must be 3 years old by their first class and be able to dance and follow instructions independently of their caregiver.

Creative Ballet (3-4yrs)

Explore the Wonderful World of Ballet

This ballet-focused class helps dancers improve strength, coordination, balance, and posture while mastering foundational steps. Students explore creativity through skill practice, dance stories, weekly activities, partnering, and improvisation. Ideal for those who thrive in a calm, focused environment, it fosters both technical development and artistic expression.




Dancers must be 4 years old by their first class to enroll.

Pre-Ballet (4-5yrs)

J Exploring the Wonderful World of Ballet

This ballet-focused class allows dancers to dive deeper into the aspects of ballet, finding their strength, coordination, balance, and posture. Dancers learn foundational ballet steps, and develop their artistry and creativity through skills practice, creative dance stories, weekly dance concept activities, partnering, improvisation, and more! This class is perfect for dancers who enjoy a quieter environment.


Dancers must be 4 years old by their first class to enroll.

Pre-Ballet & Tap (4-5yrs)

Exploring the Wonderful World of Ballet & Tap

Our Pre-Ballet & Tap class for 4-5-year-olds introduces young dancers to the basics of ballet and tap, helping develop coordination, rhythm, and motor skills. Through fun exercises, creative movement, and simple routines, children build confidence and creativity in a supportive environment. Perfect for little ones who love to move and express themselves. This class provides a delightful introduction to the world of dance.


Dancers must be 4 years old by their first class to enroll.
